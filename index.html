We know tech — So you don't have to.

Etablishing in-house IT is costly and unnecessary. Vector can provide consultation, setup, and continued support for new small business as well as support and service for existing businesses. Our services include, but are not limited to:

Hardware Deployment

Let us plan which hardware components are needed where, and execute the deployment including all the necessary training required for your employees to operate.

Networking

Vector can ensure your entire business network is communicating quickly and properly by either setting up new networking from scratch or evaluating and existing network.

Remote Access

Enable remote access to your organization's servers and software so that authorized employees can remain productive even when they can't make it to the office.

Software Deployment

Vector can conduct rollout of new software including installation and configuration, as well as removal of old software.

Backups

Your data is important, give yourself peace of mind with automatic or manual backups to offsite or onsite dedicated backup servers.

Voice over IP

Promote communication and productivity with a VoIP system custom tailored to your office and its own inbound, outbound, and internal calling needs.
